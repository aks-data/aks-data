### 🏆 1. Customer Churn Risk & RFM Segmentation Dashboard
> **Python + Power BI | Online Retail Dataset**

![Dashboard](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Completed-brightgreen?style=flat)
![Tool](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tools-Python%20%7C%20Power%20BI-blue?style=flat)

An end-to-end **Customer Analytics** project performing **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** 
segmentation to identify churn risk and revenue patterns across 4,000+ customers.

| 📌 Highlights | |
|---|---|
| 💰 Total Revenue Analyzed | ₹8.91M |
| 👥 Customers Segmented | 4,000+ |
| 🏆 Champions Revenue Share | ~45% of total |
| ⚠️ At Risk Customers Identified | 14.59% |

**Key Insights:**
- 🏆 Champions segment drives the highest revenue (~₹4M)
- ⚠️ At Risk customers flagged for urgent retention campaigns
- 📣 Actionable business recommendations derived from RFM scores
